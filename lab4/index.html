I work for a newspaper

Working for a newspaper is pretty spiffy. Rather than being a developer who writes code for the sake of there being code, there are definite non-technical stakeholders to satisfy.

If you squint your eyes and look at it backwards, providing and managing content published by a newspaper is similar in a lot of ways to providing and managing content in a library. We have taxonomists who tag all of our published assets, we have digital archives of content that isn't physically available, and we have major issues with people trying to find the content they're looking for.
